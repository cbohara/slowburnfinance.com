I'm smart and capable enough to learn machine learning but used to freeze the moment someone said "portfolio rebalancing." This blog is me getting curious about money instead of anxious about it — slowly, honestly, and in public. If money stuff makes your brain go quiet, you're in the right place.

What is this?

Slow Burn Finance is permission to start where you are.

I'm a software engineer on a FIRE path who was told I could retire at 47. Instead of just trusting the number, I decided to actually understand it. Not perform understanding — actually get there, one honest question at a time.

The freeze is real. The anxiety around money is real. And it has nothing to do with how capable you are. This is a gentle space for people who are ready to get curious anyway — without the hustle culture, without the finance bro energy, and without pretending this stuff isn't sometimes genuinely hard to sit with.

If you're building steadily toward something meaningful and want to actually understand the ground you're standing on — welcome. Go where it's warm.

Slow burn: a fire that burns steadily and sustainably — efficient, warm, and built to last. That's the idea.
